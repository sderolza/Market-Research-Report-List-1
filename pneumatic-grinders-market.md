<p><p>Pneumatic grinders are essential tools in various industries, utilizing compressed air to drive powerful grinding and finishing operations. The market is primarily divided into two types: pneumatic angle grinders and pneumatic straight grinders. Pneumatic angle grinders feature a rotating head at a right angle, ideal for reaching tight spaces and offering versatility in cutting and polishing. In contrast, pneumatic straight grinders have a straight design, suitable for precision work, fine grinding, and finishing on flat surfaces and intricate parts.</p></p>
