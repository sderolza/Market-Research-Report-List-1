<p><p>Standard reed switches in the 3W-20W range are widely used in various applications due to their compact size and reliability. The market is primarily segmented into two types: Normally Open and Normally Closed switches. Normally Open reed switches allow current to flow only when subjected to a magnetic field, making them ideal for activation applications. Conversely, Normally Closed switches maintain current flow until triggered by a magnetic field, which is useful for alarms and safety systems, offering enhanced operational flexibility.</p></p>
