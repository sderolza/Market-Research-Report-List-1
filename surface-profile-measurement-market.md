<p><strong>The Surface Profile Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stylus Surface Profiler</li><li>Non-contact Profiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface profile measurement market consists of two primary types: stylus surface profilers and non-contact profilers. Stylus profilers utilize a diamond-tipped probe that physically contacts the surface, providing high-resolution measurements of surface roughness and texture. In contrast, non-contact profilers employ advanced optical technologies, such as laser or white light interferometry, to analyze surfaces without physical contact, making them ideal for delicate or soft materials. Both methods serve various industries, ensuring precise surface characterization for quality control and research purposes.</p></p>
