<p><p>The centrifugal subsea pumps market is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for low to medium flow applications, featuring a single impeller that efficiently transfers fluids at a consistent rate. In contrast, multi-stage pumps incorporate multiple impellers, enabling them to generate higher pressures for deeper subsea operations. This diversity allows operators to select pumps based on specific depth, flow rate, and pressure requirements, optimizing performance in various subsea environments, including oil and gas exploration.</p></p>
