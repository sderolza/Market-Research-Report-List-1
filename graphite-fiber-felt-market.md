<p><strong>The Graphite Fiber Felt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN Graphite Felt</li><li>Rayon Graphite Felt</li><li>Pitch Graphite Felt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphite fiber felt market is categorized into three main types: PAN graphite felt, rayon graphite felt, and pitch graphite felt. PAN graphite felt, made from polyacrylonitrile fibers, offers high thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Rayon graphite felt, produced from viscose fibers, provides good insulation properties and flexibility. Pitch graphite felt, derived from petroleum pitch, is known for its high thermal conductivity and strength, often used in specialized industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>
