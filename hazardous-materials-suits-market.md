<p><p>The hazardous materials suits market is categorized into four levels based on protection efficacy. Level A suits provide the highest protection, featuring airtight, fully encapsulated designs for extreme hazardous environments. Level B suits offer lower protection, suitable for splash hazards with less bulk and complexity. Level C suits utilize chemical-resistant clothing with respiratory protection but lack full encapsulation, making them suitable for lesser risks. Level D suits are the least protective, used in non-hazardous environments to minimize exposure.</p></p>
