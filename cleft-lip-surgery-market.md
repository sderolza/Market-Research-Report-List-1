<p><p>Cleft lip surgery is a surgical procedure aimed at repairing a congenital deformity characterized by a split or opening in the upper lip, which can occur as a single cleft or in conjunction with a cleft palate. This surgery typically occurs when the child is between three to six months old, although it can be performed later if necessary. The objective is to restore normal lip appearance and function, enhance feeding, and facilitate normal speech development.</p><p>The Cleft Lip Surgery Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period.
