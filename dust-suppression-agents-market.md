<p><ul><li>Wet Dust Suppression Agents</li><li>Dry Dust Suppression Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dust suppression agents market is divided into two primary types: wet dust suppression agents and dry dust suppression agents. Wet dust suppression agents involve the application of liquid solutions, often containing water, chemicals, or polymers, to bind dust particles and prevent them from becoming airborne. In contrast, dry dust suppression agents consist of granular or powder formulations, such as lignosulfonates or various binding agents, that are spread on surfaces to absorb moisture and minimize dust generation. Both types are essential for controlling dust emissions in various industries.</p></p>
