<p><strong>The Servo Drivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse</li><li>Analog</li><li>Network</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The servo drivers market is primarily categorized into three types: pulse, analog, and network. Pulse servo drivers utilize step and direction signals for precise control of motors, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision. Analog servo drivers, on the other hand, use voltage or current to regulate motor performance, offering smooth operation for less demanding tasks. Network servo drivers enable communication over industrial networks, allowing for centralized control and advanced features like diagnostics, making them suitable for complex automation systems.</p></p>
