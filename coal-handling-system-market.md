Reclaimers retrieve stored coal for processing and transport. Feeders regulate the flow of coal into machinery, ensuring consistent supply. Conveyors transport coal between different stages of handling, minimizing manual intervention. Additionally, other equipment may include dust suppression systems and weighbridges, all contributing to a streamlined process in coal storage and transportation.</p></p>
