<p><p>The PTFE processing materials market comprises various types, including porous products, sheets, skived films, rods, and pipes/sleeves. Porous products are used for filtration and insulation applications. Sheets are versatile for sealing and gasket applications, while skived films offer flexibility and thin profiles for specialized uses. Rods are suitable for mechanical components due to their strength and durability, and pipes/sleeves are essential for chemical transport and protection. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, highlighting PTFE's versatility.</p></p>
