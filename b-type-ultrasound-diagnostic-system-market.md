<p><p>The B-type Ultrasound Diagnostic System market features three primary scanning types: fanshaped scanning, linear scanning, and mixed type. Fanshaped scanning utilizes a wide-angle approach, offering extensive field coverage ideal for larger areas. Linear scanning provides high-resolution images in a straight line, focusing on detailed views of specific organs or structures. The mixed type combines both techniques, leveraging their advantages to enhance diagnostic accuracy and versatility. Each method serves unique clinical needs, contributing to the overall growth of the ultrasound market.</p></p>
