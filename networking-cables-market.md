<p><p>The networking cables market comprises three primary types: coaxial cables, twisted pair cables, and optical fiber cables. Coaxial cables are widely used for cable television and internet connections due to their resistance to electromagnetic interference. Twisted pair cables, including unshielded and shielded variations, are commonly utilized in computer networks for their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Optical fiber cables offer high-speed data transmission over long distances, making them essential for advanced telecommunication and data networks, especially in urban areas.</p></p>
