<p><p>The Acoustic Emission (AE) Sensors market applications are primarily divided into Burst Acoustic Emission and Continuous Acoustic Emission. Burst AE involves detecting short, high-amplitude sound waves generated by rapid stress release events, ideal for monitoring materials in critical structures like bridges or pipelines. Continuous AE focuses on real-time monitoring of ongoing acoustic signals, allowing for the gradual tracking of structural integrity in various engineering applications. Both approaches enhance predictive maintenance and safety across industries, including manufacturing and civil engineering.</p></p>
