<p><p>Glass Fused to Steel Tanks are categorized into two market types: Two-Layer Sheet and Single-Layer Sheet. The Two-Layer Sheet tanks consist of a steel core coated with a layer of glass, providing enhanced durability and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, Single-Layer Sheet tanks feature a single layer of glass-fused steel, typically used in less demanding environments. Both types are valued for their strength, longevity, and ability to withstand harsh conditions.</p></p>
