<p><p>The coal handling equipment market encompasses two primary types: material handling and crushing equipment. Material handling equipment includes systems that transport, store, and manage coal, ensuring efficient movement from extraction to processing. This includes conveyors, stackers, and reclaimers. Crushing equipment, on the other hand, is designed to reduce the size of coal, facilitating easier handling and processing. It includes crushers and grinders that enhance coal's usability in energy production and other industrial applications, optimizing operational efficiency in coal processing plants.</p></p>
