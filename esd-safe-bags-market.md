<p><strong>The ESD-Safe Bags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antistatic Bags</li><li>Dissipative Bags</li><li>Metallized Bags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ESD-safe bags market consists of three primary types: antistatic bags, dissipative bags, and metallized bags. Antistatic bags are designed to prevent static charge buildup and protect sensitive electronics. Dissipative bags allow controlled dissipation of charges while providing a moderate level of static protection. Metallized bags offer a high level of electrostatic shielding by incorporating a metalized layer, making them ideal for high-value or sensitive components. Each type is tailored to specific applications within the electronics and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
