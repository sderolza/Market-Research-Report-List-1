<p><p>Suspension markets are categorized into three main types: passive, semi-active, and active suspension systems. Passive suspension offers fixed damping characteristics, providing basic ride comfort. Semi-active suspension adjusts its damping in response to road conditions, enhancing comfort and handling without complex mechanisms. Active suspension systems utilize real-time sensors and actuators to actively control wheel motion, delivering superior ride quality and stability. Each type balances performance, comfort, and technology, catering to various consumer needs in the automotive sector.</p></p>
