<p><p>Textile shredders for recycling are classified into three main types: single shaft, double shaft, and others. Single shaft shredders utilize one blade rotor, ideal for uniform shredding and handling lower volumes of textiles. Double shaft shredders contain two shafts, allowing for more efficient processing of thicker materials and higher volumes. Other types may include specialized machines designed for specific textile recycling needs, such as pre-shredders or granulators, enhancing versatility and adaptability in textile waste management.</p></p>
