<p><strong>The Short Radar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CW Radars</li><li>Pulsed Radars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The short radar system market comprises two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) radars and pulsed radars. CW radars emit a continuous signal and are primarily used for speed detection and tracking applications, offering high accuracy in measuring velocities. In contrast, pulsed radars transmit short bursts of radio waves, enabling range detection and target identification by measuring the time delay of returned signals. Both systems are essential in diverse sectors, including automotive, defense, and industrial automation, enhancing object detection and surveillance capabilities.</p></p>
