<p><ul><li>Single Air Valves</li><li>Double Air Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air valve market comprises primarily two types: single air valves and double air valves. Single air valves are designed to release air from a pipeline and are commonly used in water distribution systems, ensuring efficient flow and preventing air blockage. In contrast, double air valves serve a dual function; they permit air to escape and allow for air intake, making them suitable for larger pipelines and more complex systems. Both types enhance operational efficiency and reduce maintenance issues in fluid distribution systems.</p></p>
