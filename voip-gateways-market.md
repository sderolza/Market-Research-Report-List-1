<p><strong>The VoIP Gateways Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog VoIP Gateway</li><li>Digital VoIP Gateway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VoIP gateways serve as vital interfaces between traditional telephony systems and modern VoIP networks. Analog VoIP gateways convert analog voice signals into digital data for transmission over the internet, making them ideal for connecting legacy phone systems to VoIP networks. In contrast, digital VoIP gateways facilitate communication between digital telephony systems, such as ISDN or T1 lines, and VoIP networks. Both types enable seamless integration, enhancing communication efficiency and reducing costs for businesses migrating to VoIP technologies.</p></p>
