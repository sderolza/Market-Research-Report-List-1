<p><strong>The Edge Routers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subscriber Edge Router</li><li>Label Edge Router</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge routers are crucial in telecommunications, connecting subscribers to the core network. The Subscriber Edge Router primarily serves residential and business customers, managing data traffic and ensuring efficient internet access. In contrast, the Label Edge Router focuses on MPLS (Multiprotocol Label Switching) technology, directing packets based on labels rather than IP addresses. This enhances speed and reliability in data transmission. Both types play vital roles in improving network performance and user experience in increasingly complex communication infrastructures.</p></p>
