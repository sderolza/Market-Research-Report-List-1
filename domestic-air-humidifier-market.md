<p><p>The domestic air humidifier market features several types, including ultrasonic, hot steam, and pure humidifiers. Ultrasonic models use high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist, providing a gentle and energy-efficient way to humidify air. Hot steam humidifiers boil water to produce steam, offering effective moisture but consuming more energy. Pure humidifiers focus on delivering clean, purified moisture, often incorporating filtration systems to ensure the air is not only humidified but also free from impurities and allergens.</p></p>
