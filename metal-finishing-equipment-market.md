<p><p>The metal finishing equipment market comprises three main types: inorganic metal finishing, which involves processes using non-carbon-based compounds such as acids and salts for surface treatment; organometallic finishing, where organic compounds containing metal are utilized for specialized applications and enhanced properties; and mixed metal finishing, combining both inorganic and organometallic methods to achieve superior surface characteristics and improved performance. These categories cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing metal durability, aesthetic appeal, and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
