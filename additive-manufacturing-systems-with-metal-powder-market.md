<p><p>Additive manufacturing systems using metal powder encompass various techniques for layer-by-layer production of components. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) utilizes a laser to fuse metal powder, creating high-quality, complex geometries. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) employs an electron beam in a vacuum for melting metal, suitable for high-performance applications. Other methods include binder jetting and direct energy deposition, each offering unique advantages in terms of speed, material compatibility, and precision, catering to diverse industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
