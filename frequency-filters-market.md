<p><strong>The Frequency Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Frequency Filters</li><li>Active Frequency Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frequency filters are vital in signal processing, and they are categorized into two main market types: passive and active frequency filters. Passive filters, composed of resistors, capacitors, and inductors, rely on passive components to attenuate unwanted frequencies without amplification. Active filters incorporate operational amplifiers and other active components, enabling them to provide gain and better performance, such as sharper cut-offs. Both filter types find applications in various industries, including telecommunications, audio equipment, and electronics, enhancing signal quality and performance.</p></p>
