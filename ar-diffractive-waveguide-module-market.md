<p><p>The AR Diffractive Waveguide Module market is segmented into two primary types: Surface Relief Grating and Volumetric Holographic Grating. Surface Relief Grating utilizes micro-structured patterns on a surface to manipulate light direction, promoting compact and efficient designs suitable for augmented reality applications. In contrast, Volumetric Holographic Grating employs three-dimensional holographic patterns embedded in a medium to diffract light, creating rich visual experiences. Both types offer unique advantages for AR devices, influencing design choices based on performance and application needs.</p></p>
