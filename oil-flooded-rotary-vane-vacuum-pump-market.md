<p><p>The oil-flooded rotary vane vacuum pump market is segmented into single-stage and two-stage rotary vane vacuum pumps. Single-stage rotary vane vacuum pumps operate with one set of vanes, providing mid-level vacuum performance suitable for various applications. In contrast, two-stage rotary vane vacuum pumps utilize two sets of vanes, achieving lower ultimate pressures and higher vacuum levels, making them ideal for demanding industrial processes. Both types leverage oil flooding for lubrication, enhancing efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
