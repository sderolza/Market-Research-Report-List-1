<p><ul><li>Type N</li><li>Type S</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Masonry White Cement market is primarily categorized into two types: Type N and Type S. Type N is designed for general masonry applications, offering a balance of strength and workability, making it suitable for both load-bearing and non-load-bearing structures. Type S, on the other hand, is ideal for structural applications requiring higher strength and enhanced bonding properties, making it suitable for reinforced masonry. Both types cater to specific construction needs and are valued for their aesthetics and durability.</p></p>
