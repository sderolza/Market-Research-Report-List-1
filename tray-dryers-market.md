<p><p>Tray dryers are classified based on their heating mediums: Thermic Fluid, Steam, Hot Air, and Electricity. Thermic Fluid dryers utilize heated liquids to transfer warm air for efficient drying. Steam dryers deliver steam to elevate temperature and remove moisture. Hot Air dryers circulate heated air, suitable for various materials. Electric dryers employ electrical resistance to generate heat directly, offering precise temperature control. Each type caters to different industrial needs, focusing on efficiency, energy usage, and product suitability.</p></p>
