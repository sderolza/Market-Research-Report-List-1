<p><strong>The Automotive Flasher Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Flasher</li><li>Thermal Flasher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flasher module market consists of two primary types: electronic flashers and thermal flashers. Electronic flashers utilize solid-state components for precise control and reliability, making them suitable for modern vehicles with advanced lighting systems. In contrast, thermal flashers rely on a bimetallic strip that heats up to create a delay in the signaling process, commonly found in older vehicles. Both types serve to manage turn signal and hazard light operations, ensuring vehicle safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
