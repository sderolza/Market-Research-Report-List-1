<p><p>The electric well pumps market is primarily divided into two types: open well and borewell pumps. Open well pumps are designed for shallow water extraction and are typically used in agricultural and residential applications, offering high capacity and easy maintenance. Borewell pumps, on the other hand, are suited for deeper water sources and are more efficient in areas with limited water supply. Both types play a crucial role in irrigation and water management, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
