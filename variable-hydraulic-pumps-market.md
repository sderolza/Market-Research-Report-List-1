Axial pumps utilize a design where the fluid flows parallel to the drive shaft, providing high efficiency and compact size, making them suitable for mobile equipment. Radial pumps, on the other hand, direct fluid flow perpendicular to the shaft, offering robustness and reliability for industrial applications. Both types serve diverse sectors, enhancing system performance, energy efficiency, and responsiveness in hydraulic applications.</p></p>
